Fetch Cryptlib (http://www.bebits.com/app/3682).

To compile cryptlib on Zeta (BONE systems?) you need to follow the steps details in the Talkback section
(http://www.bebits.com/talkback/3682). Specifically you need add "-lbind -lsocket" to line 1203 of the 
Makefile. Then do a make shared

Copy the resulting libcl.so to ~/config/lib.

Copy the headers (crypt.h, cryptacd.h etc) to ~/config/include/cryptlib

The addon should then compile
